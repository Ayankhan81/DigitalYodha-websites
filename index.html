Why Digital Safety Is

A National Need

India's rapid digital growth has transformed education, governance, healthcare, and commerce.
Digital safety is a national need because it protects citizens from severe risks like financial fraud, data theft, identity theft,
and cyberbullying However, this progress has a shadow


The rise in Cyber risks- fraud, misinformtaion, data misuse, and exploitation--
threatning the safety of millions who are coming online for the first time.

"Technology should empower, not endager"
