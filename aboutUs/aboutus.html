Who We Are

Grassroots Action for Digital Safety

Digital Yodha Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to building a cyber-safe, digitally empowered India. We work at the grassroots level to spread digital literacy and responsible online behavior among citizens across all age groups.


Founded with the belief that awareness is the first line of defense, we bridge the gap between rapid technological growth and society's preparedness to handle it.

Why We Exist

India’s digital revolution has transformed how we live, but it has led to a sharp rise in cyber fraud, data privacy violations, and digital exploitation. Cyber safety education remains limited for many.

Cyber fraud and online scams
Data privacy violations
Social media misuse
Exploitation of vulnerable groups

"We exist to ensure that no citizen is left unprotected in the digital age."

Mission

To empower individuals and communities with knowledge, tools, and confidence to navigate the digital world safely, responsibly, and ethically.

Vision

To create an India where every citizen understands digital risks, practices cyber hygiene, and knows how to respond to cyber threats.
